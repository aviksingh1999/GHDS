Dharmic Naujawaan


The Dharmic Naujawaan is the central youth arm of the GHDS, a Non-Governmental Organization registered under a Trust Deed. The group was revived on 01st October, 2000 with the mission of providing support to the GHDS in the fulfillment of its goals and in particular, foster the development of Hindu youths to meaningfully contribute towards the development of Guyana, through the initiation, facilitation, sponsorship, propagation and implementation of religious, educational, cultural and social activities.
